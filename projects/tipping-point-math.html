--- layout: project title: Tipping Point Math ---

An educational Youtube Channel dedicated to sparking interest in math. Sponsored and founded by Professor Chamberland

The Motivation: There are a variety of beautiful and surprising results in mathematics which are completely accessible to anyone who has completed a high school level of education. Tipping Point Math aims to bring the joys of learning about math to a greater audience, showing math as you never imagined.

The Channel: A team of four students and professor Chamberland write scripts and put together corresponding videos on a variety of mathematical subjects. New videos are produced roughly once a month.

The Tools: FinalCut

My role: Writer
